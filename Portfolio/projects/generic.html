Project Overview- VKL Beauty House

A small startup located in Louisiana, contacted me to establish their web presence with a website to identify their brand as well as incorporating ecommerce functionality.

Role

Designer and Developer

I was contacted as the designer and developer for this project. I had communicated with the owner remotely for a year to develop and administer the site until they were ready to launch and take the reins of the site themself.

This was a great opportunity to express my skill in web design and developmentin order to create a useful product. Not to mention the creation process was quite enjoyable.

I had to to a lot of research on color theory and design font. As a self taught developer/designer, a lot of the concepts were new but i was able to quickly understand them within the first week of starting the project to produce a beautiful design.

Features

This site was created as a web store designed and developed in Wix CMS. It incorporated the Wiz eCommerce API, secure login as well as intergrated external links to client social media profiles.
